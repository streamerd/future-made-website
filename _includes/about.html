About the imaginable(s)

Future, Made. is an independent Art & Technology collective hoping to be creative in what, how, and why it exists. Opted for making a positive impact via progressively conducting a set of experimental projects in the socio-economical and political aspects of a multi-lateral, inclusive and globally designable future; while fostering commons-making and skills-making, around new socio-economical value chains with humanitarian values in a grasroots fashion.

We are making an open call for all individuals and communities of the world; who are into ideation, design, research, and development activities on observing the civic engagements and the impact of demand-side-initiated future scenarios (aka. demand-augmentation) and creating opportunities to extend the Freedom of Expression with Expression of Demand in several fields.


Future seems to be observable, when there is an observable future-oriented intention bases exist. Otherwise, there will be an ongoing need for historical-data that is being collected by some, in real-time. We should be ready for a future, where all ads are blocked because of the known and being experienced/forced, bad practices. Our technology-enabled projects have also inspired from Community Supported Agriculture, where a future clarity is given to farmers by the consumers.

Making experiences, related services hence in the long term, nconditional income more demandable with an approach that incentivices audience to declare their demand via democratic, fair, location-independent and participatory mechanisms. between producer/creator/seller and consumer/audience/client of things, places and most importantly, experiences.

All concepts and projects are being developed to further increase the accessibility, sustainability of the Arts, Culture and Knowlegde, opening new channels for expressing local and global problems via causes included to improve the ability of people to organize and demand changes in an non-anthropocentric manner, with an initial dedication on fostering green, safe, localized, decentralize-able and deferrable art events of the future, by design.

Hence, efforts started with improving the, deterministic and risk-taking performances ticketing/planning methodologies. and creating future-oriented, temporary and/or long-term networks via creating micro-incentives and multi-lateral beneficiary sharings between participants.
